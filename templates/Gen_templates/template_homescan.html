SCAN is our first education based product which is a result of continuous research and innovation. The aim of this product is to provide a healthy environment to students, teachers and parents which will be helpful in overall development of child. Our vision is that every child in the country should get ample of opportunities for his development and his best qualities should be known at early stages so that they can be developed to professional level.

With this product we are targeting to change the concept of Information to knowledge and study to learning. We have also taken care that we give ample of respect to all the teachers involved with child and that teacher-child relationship becomes stronger with Indian education entering a new era.

Read an overview of SCAN

SCAN is an Artificially Intelligent system that prepares itself uniquely for every child so that he can be assessed as per his qualities. It is not just an assessment prepared by a group of experts in which a set of questions are designed and result is analyzed.

But we at Critical Velos strongly believe that along with experts the role of teacher directly associated with a student cannot be neglected and their inputs are necessary to assess a child and make a plan for them. Hence we have come up with a unique system that is embedded with inputs of experts and will also take inputs from teachers that are directly associated with students.

Based on these inputs every child is assessed uniquely at regular intervals and it helps in knowing his mistake at early stages and work on them before they turn into his weakness.

Thus SCAN gives special care every child and helps him building his own dream future.
